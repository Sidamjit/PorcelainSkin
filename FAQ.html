Laser Hair Removal

Everything you need to know before starting your long-term hair reduction journey.

Is laser hair removal painful?

Clients usually describe a warm flicking sensation. We adjust the settings for your comfort, and use cooling to keep the experience pleasant.

Can all skin tones be treated?

Yes. Our devices have multiple settings to safely treat a wide range of skin types. Your consultation confirms suitability and the safest parameters.

Pre-care

  • Shave the treatment area 24 hours beforehand.
  • Avoid waxing, epilating, or depilatory creams for three to four weeks prior.

Aftercare

  • Avoid heat, intense exercise, active skincare, and sun exposure for 48 to 72 hours.
  • Apply SPF 50 daily and moisturise gently.

What skin concerns can you help with?

What facial should I choose?

Do you offer courses?

Aftercare

Brows, Lashes & Eye Enhancements

Define and enhance your natural features with precision brow and lash artistry.

Lash lift vs extensions?

A lash lift elevates and sets your own lashes for up to six to eight weeks. Extensions add length or volume with individual fibres and require refills every two to three weeks.

Do you offer brow threading?

Yes. Threading and waxing are both available for precise brow and facial hair grooming.

Do you do cosmetic tattooing?

Yes. Powder brow and lip blush services begin with a detailed consultation so we can tailor shape, tone, and aftercare.

Waxing

Smooth results for face and body with careful pre and post-care guidance.

How long should the hair be?

Around three to four weeks of growth (roughly a grain of rice) is ideal so the wax grips evenly and results last.

Aftercare

Avoid heat, exercise, friction, tight clothing, and active products for 24 hours to keep skin calm.

What massage styles are available?

Session lengths

Massage appointments are available in 30, 45, 60, and 90 minute durations depending on the focus you need.

Timing, prep & aftercare

  • Waxing 3-4 weeks growth, gentle exfoliation before, avoid heat/exercise for 24 hours.
  • Laser hair removal Shave 24 hours before, no waxing for 3-4 weeks prior, SPF 50 daily afterwards.
  • Facials, peels & IPL Pause strong actives 3-5 days either side, SPF 50 daily, avoid heat and actives for 48-72 hours.
  • Lash lift/extensions Arrive with clean eyes, avoid water/steam for 24 hours after your appointment.
  • Manicure & pedicure Arrive with bare nails where possible; advise if removal time is required.
