Frequently asked questions

How do I know which service to book?

Start with a Skin Strategy Consultation. We’ll map your skin, talk through goals, and recommend the right sequence of facials, needling, or laser therapies.

Are treatments safe for sensitive skin?

Absolutely. We adapt active ingredients, machine settings and treatment timing based on your skin health, heritage and comfort level.

Can I combine services in one visit?

Yes — combination appointments are our speciality. We’ll guide you on the best pairings, plus which enhancements deliver the biggest glow for your time.
