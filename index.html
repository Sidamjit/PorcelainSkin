Is laser hair removal painful?

Most clients describe a warm flicking sensation. We tailor the energy and cooling to your skin tone and hair type so every pass stays comfortable while still delivering results.

How many sessions will I need?

Most areas require six to ten sessions spaced four to six weeks apart. Because hair growth cycles differ, your therapist will outline a personalised schedule and maintenance plan.

Can I treat my skin with active acne, redness, or pigmentation?

Yes. We offer acne support programmes, pigmentation correction, and laser rejuvenation to calm inflammation, refine texture, and brighten tone. Your consultation confirms the safest treatment path and home care.

Do you offer manicures and pedicures?

Absolutely. Our manicure and pedicure services focus on nail health, precision shaping, hydration, and impeccably clean finishes. Spa-level rituals are available when you want a deeper unwind.

What is the difference between a lash lift and lash extensions?

A lash lift curls and lifts your natural lashes for up to six to eight weeks. Extensions add tailored length or volume with individual fibres and require refills every two to three weeks to stay full.

Do you offer brows and threading?

Yes. We sculpt brows through waxing or threading, offer brow and lash tinting, and create bespoke brow designs that enhance your facial structure with soft, fuller definition.

What should I avoid before and after skin or laser treatments?

Skip sun exposure, self-tan, saunas, and active skincare (retinol or strong exfoliants) for at least 48 hours on either side. For laser hair removal, shave beforehand and avoid waxing or epilating during the course.

Are treatment plans available?

Yes. We frequently create structured plans for acne, pigmentation correction, skin rejuvenation, and long-term hair reduction. Your therapist will tailor the timeline, modalities, and home products to your goals.
